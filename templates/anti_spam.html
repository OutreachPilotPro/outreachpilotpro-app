Anti-Spam Policy

We are committed to preventing spam and ensuring responsible email practices. Learn about our anti-spam measures and compliance.

Our Commitment to Anti-Spam

OutreachPilotPro is committed to preventing spam and promoting responsible email marketing practices. We comply with all applicable anti-spam laws and regulations, including the CAN-SPAM Act, GDPR, and other international anti-spam legislation.

What is Spam?

Spam is unsolicited commercial email sent in bulk. It typically includes:

  • Emails sent without recipient consent
  • Misleading subject lines or sender information
  • Lack of proper unsubscribe mechanisms
  • False or misleading content
  • Emails sent to purchased or harvested email lists

⚠️ Zero Tolerance for Spam

We have a zero-tolerance policy for spam. Any user found to be sending spam through our platform will have their account immediately suspended and may face legal consequences.

Our Anti-Spam Measures

We implement comprehensive measures to prevent spam and ensure compliance:

  • Email Verification: All email addresses are verified before sending
  • Consent Management: Require explicit consent for all email communications
  • Unsubscribe Compliance: All emails include clear unsubscribe options
  • Sender Authentication: Implement SPF, DKIM, and DMARC protocols
  • Content Filtering: Monitor email content for spam indicators
  • Rate Limiting: Prevent bulk sending abuse
  • Reputation Monitoring: Track sender reputation and deliverability

CAN-SPAM Act Compliance

We ensure full compliance with the CAN-SPAM Act requirements:

  • Accurate Header Information: All emails contain accurate sender information
  • Non-Deceptive Subject Lines: Subject lines accurately reflect email content
  • Clear Identification: Emails are clearly identified as advertisements
  • Physical Address: Include valid physical postal address
  • Unsubscribe Mechanism: Provide clear and functional unsubscribe options
  • Prompt Unsubscribe Processing: Honor unsubscribe requests within 10 business days

✅ Best Practices We Enforce

  • Always obtain explicit consent before sending emails
  • Use clear, honest subject lines
  • Include physical address in all commercial emails
  • Provide one-click unsubscribe functionality
  • Respect unsubscribe requests immediately
  • Maintain clean, permission-based email lists
  • Monitor and maintain good sender reputation

User Responsibilities

As a user of OutreachPilotPro, you agree to:

  • Only send emails to recipients who have given explicit consent
  • Maintain accurate and up-to-date email lists
  • Include proper unsubscribe mechanisms in all emails
  • Honor unsubscribe requests promptly
  • Use accurate sender information
  • Comply with all applicable anti-spam laws
  • Not use our service for sending spam or unsolicited emails

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Sending emails without recipient consent
  • Using purchased or harvested email lists
  • Falsifying sender information or headers
  • Using misleading subject lines
  • Failing to include unsubscribe options
  • Ignoring unsubscribe requests
  • Sending emails to invalid or non-existent addresses
  • Using our service for phishing or fraudulent activities

Monitoring and Enforcement

We actively monitor email sending patterns and take action against violations:

  • Automated Monitoring: Real-time monitoring of sending patterns
  • Complaint Tracking: Monitor spam complaints and bounce rates
  • Account Suspension: Immediate suspension for spam violations
  • Legal Action: Cooperation with law enforcement when necessary
  • Reporting: Report violations to relevant authorities

Reporting Spam

If you receive spam or suspect spam activity, please report it immediately:

  • Forward the spam email to spam@outreachpilotpro.com
  • Include the full email headers if possible
  • Provide any additional context or information
  • We investigate all reports promptly and take appropriate action
